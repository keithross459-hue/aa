# 30-Day Growth Roadmap

## Week 1: Launch Signal

- Launch on `fiilthy.ai` with Pro and CEO plans.
- Run founder-led demos on TikTok, X, LinkedIn, and YouTube Shorts.
- Offer first 100 users a tracked coupon.
- Push affiliate/referral link to every activated user.

## Week 2: Conversion

- Review free to paid conversion in PostHog.
- A/B test homepage headline and pricing CTA.
- Add abandoned checkout and upgrade prompt campaigns.
- Interview first 10 paying users.

## Week 3: Retention

- Build lifecycle emails around first product, first launch, first sale, and first referral.
- Add cohort report review every Monday.
- Identify top niches/products and create public templates.

## Week 4: Scale

- Recruit affiliates with milestone bonuses.
- Publish case studies from best early launches.
- Add paid retargeting from PostHog/Meta audiences.
- Decide whether to migrate CRA to Vite and add Redis-backed queues/rate limits.
